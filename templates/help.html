Scoreboard shortcut help    


Start / Stop

To use the keyboard shortcuts keys are underscored, e.g. "n" starts and stops the clock.

Shot Clock controls

Running Clock Reset. "Y"
Pause Clock Reset. "H"
Shot taken reset. "U"
Corner reset. "J"

Penalty / Major / Goal

Press the action key then select player number for home team or cntl player number for away team.
For Goal press "V"
For Majors press "C"
For Penalties press "B"
For Card press "F"

For Home Timeout press "R", for Away timeout press (T) .

Players

For player numbers 1-9, use the top row including the "y" for 10 "u" for 11 and "i" for 12 and "c" for 13

Players & Referees

At the begining enter the player and referee details. If you need to edit the details, edit them, then press finish, and it will reprint the report in the bottom of the file.
