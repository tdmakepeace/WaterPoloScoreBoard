Scoreboard  Help    

Start / Stop

To use the keyboard shortcuts keys are underscored, e.g. "s" starts and stops the clock.

Timeout / Penalty / Major / Goal

For "home" press the key, eg "g" for home goal, then select player number.

For "away" hold down ctrl and press the key, eg ctrl + "g" for away goal, then select player number.

Players

For player numbers 1-9, use the top row including the "0" for 10 "-" for 11 and "=" for 12 and "]" for 13

Players & Referees

At the begining enter the player and referee details. If you need to edit the details, edit them, then press finish, and it will reprint the report in the bottom of the file.
